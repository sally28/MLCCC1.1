Welcome to Main Line Chinese Culture Center

About School

Established in 2003, the Main Line Chinese Culture Center, Inc. (MLCCC, 明朗中国文化中心) is a nonprofit, nonpartisan, nonpolitical charity organization whose mission is to promote and preserve Chinese heritage as a cultural ambassador through our Weekend School, Immersive Summer Camp and Community Services within the Greater Philadelphia community.
